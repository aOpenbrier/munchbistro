Munch Bistro's first mission is to create pure, traditional, classic Thai dishes which are as authentic as any you would find in the night markets of Bangkok or Phuket. At the same time, to find new ways to approach Thai food with innovative and creative recipes that combine our customers’ favorite flavors of Thai food with some of their most beloved cuisine from other parts of the world.

Whether you are an omnivore, vegetarian, or vegan… whether you are a fan of our 33 craft beers on tap, our saké and wine options, or our unique SABÉ cocktails… whether you favor timeless classics like Pad Thai, Pad See Ew and Green Curry... or you want to try something completely unique like our Tom Yum Flatbread Pizza or our Orange Chicken Sandwich, we will offer you the freshest, tastiest, and most delicious authentic Thai food and Thai inspired dishes in Orange County.
